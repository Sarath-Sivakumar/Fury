Fury

Fury is a mobile finance management app designed to help it's users track their expenses,earnings,budget and dues and debt in one place.its key features include:

1.Expense tracker: Users can easily log their daily expenses and categorize them for better organization and analysis.The app also users to set budgets for different expense categories and track their spending against those budgets.

2.Earnings tracker: Users can input their Income sources and track their earnings over time. The app can also generate income reports and provide insights into income trends.

3.Budget manager: Users can create and manage their budgets for different time periods,such as monthly or yearly. The app also provides notifications and alerts to help users stay on track with their budget goals.

4.Dues and debt manager. Users can keep track of their debts and loans, and set reminders for upcoming payments. The app can also help users create a payoff plan for their debts.

Overall, Fury aims to simplify the financial management process for users and provide a comprehensive solution for tracking their finances.
