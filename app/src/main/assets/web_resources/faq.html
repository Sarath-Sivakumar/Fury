Common Questions

  • Is setting a budget mandatory?

    No, but we always recommend you to set a budget and track it inorder to ensure financial stability.

  • How can I clear all data & create new?

    Clear all data option can be accessed from utilities section of the settings.

Expense Tracker

  • Where can I see my previous expenses?

    All your expenses can be accessed from the 'View All' option available on the right side of top categories in dashboard.

  • How can I see detailed info of my expenses?

    On clicking on each item from expense list you can see the details of that expense item.

  • How can I delete a wrongly added expense?

    Swiping on either side of the expense item will delete them.

  • Why does my current average not showing?

    Current average is calculated after necessary data has been collected for 7 consecutive days.

Earnings Tracker

  • Can I edit already added earnings amount?

    Sorry, as of now we don't support editing amount.But you can still edit the source name and other of information previously provided by clicking on the item.

  • What does the pay period mean?

    pay period means the frequency at which the earnings is repeated.It is basically for recurring earnings like Salary and others. For non-recurring earnings you can chose onetime option

  • What does the credit mode means?

    It is the way that you received the earnings.It cash be either in cash or bank

  • Why does my daily average not showing?

    Daily average is calculated after necessary data has been collected for 7 consecutive days.

Budget

  • How can I manually create a budget?

    On budget creating popup, under the continue option manual budgeting option is available.

  • What does the ideal limit mean?

    It the highest amount you can spend in a day according to your budget.

  • What does the credit mode means?

    It is the way that you collected the earnings.It cash be either in cash or bank

  • What does current average mean?

    Current average is your current daily average expense.It may fluctuate in many cases.

Dues & Debt

  • How can I mark a due as paid?

    Swipe left on the due to mark it as paid

  • How can I add repeating dues?

    Select the 'repeat on this day' option while adding a due.

  • Where can I access all dues including paid?

    All your dues can be viewed by the 'View All' option available on the right side of Dues Overview in dashboard.
